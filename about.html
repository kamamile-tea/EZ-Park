Our Mission

EZ Park strives to provide a more seamless experience for drivers with city lifestyles. As popular as the subway systems are, 45% of the households in New York City have a car! As a company based in NYC, we understand the difficulty of owning a car in such a busy urban area and we aim to offer the best parking experience for all city residents.



Company History

EZ Park was established in February 2021 by four Diverse Influencers fellows who wanted to make life in urban areas easier on those who don’t always take the subway everyday. Living in bustling New York City, parking is something that can easily ruin a perfectly planned day. Recognizing the impact that this has on drivers everywhere, we created EZ Park to ensure that taking a drive doesn’t mean taking away from the experience.
